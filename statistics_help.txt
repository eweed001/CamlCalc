Statistics Functions:
- mean
- median
- range
- standard deviation ("stdev")
- min 
- max

These functions can all take in inputs as a list of numbers in
the form of [1 2 3 4 ....] 
ex.
mean [1 2 3 4]

or a predefined list in the form of let s = [1 2 3 ...] 
ex. 
let s = [1 2 3 4]
mean s

- permutation ("perm")
- combination ("comb")

These functions are applied to two arguments, being the n and r
in nPr and nCr respectively 


The result of all these functions can be bound to variable name using a let 
definition
